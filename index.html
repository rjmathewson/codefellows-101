More about Mariners Hall of Famers

The Seattle Mariners Hall of Fame lineup is led by Ken Griffey Jr. and Lou Piniella.

Ken Griffey Jr. aka "The Kid" was a first round draft pick of the Seattle Mariners in 1987.

Lou Piniella joined the Seattle Mariners in 1993.
